The Method

The Bohème Evening Ritual

Four unhurried steps. Twenty minutes. A practice that transforms routine into ceremony.

01

Cleanse with Intention

Warm water, circular motions. Let the day dissolve slowly — this is not a chore, it is a transition.

02

Tone & Awaken

Apply Forêt Tonic with open palms, pressing gently into skin. Breathe in the forest.

03

Treat with Serum

Two drops of Terre Serum, warmed between fingertips. Press, don't rub. Let it sink.

04

Seal with Oil

Three drops of Lumière Oil to seal and illuminate. Massage upward with slow, deliberate strokes.

Botanical Index

Nature's most
potent gifts.

Every ingredient is selected for clinical efficacy and ethical sourcing. We work directly with small farms, co-operatives, and wild harvesters across twelve countries. Nothing is anonymous.

Rose Hip Seed

Rosa canina

Cold-pressed from wild roses in the Andes. Rich in trans-retinoic acid and essential fatty acids — a natural brightener and elasticity restorer.

Sea Buckthorn

Hippophae rhamnoides

Hand-harvested in the Tibetan Plateau. The highest natural source of Omega-7 — repairs the skin barrier and restores a youthful, lit-from-within glow.

Neroli Blossom

Citrus aurantium

Steam-distilled from bitter orange blossoms in Tunisia. A natural toner with regenerative properties — calms redness and evens skin tone over time.

Bakuchiol

Psoralea corylifolia

Sourced from sacred babchi seeds in India. A plant-based retinol alternative — proven to reduce fine lines without the irritation or sensitivity.

Olive Squalane

Olea europaea

Sustainably derived from Spanish olive harvest. A near-perfect mimic of skin's own sebum — lightweight, non-comedogenic, deeply nourishing.

Turmeric Root

Curcuma longa

Wild-grown in the forests of Kerala. Curcumin is one of nature's most potent anti-inflammatories — it calms, clears, and gently illuminates the complexion.
